Courses We Offer

CSS, HUMS, EIM, HE, ABM

CSS

Computer System Servicing NC2 is a Philippines vocational program covering hardware/software maintenance. Graduates can work as computer technicians.

HUMS

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) study human behavior, societies, and cultures through subjects like literature, history, philosophy, psychology, and more.
It emphasizes critical thinking and analysis, preparing students for careers in education, research, and public service.

EIM

Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) involves installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical systems and equipment. Professionals ensure the safe and efficient functioning of electrical components,
including wiring, lighting, and various devices. EIM covers both preventive maintenance and troubleshooting for optimal system performance.

HE

Home Economics is a field of study focusing on practical life skills for managing a household. It includes areas such as cooking, nutrition,
textiles, clothing, family dynamics, personal finance, and interior design. The aim is to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed for everyday living and well-being.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) is an academic strand in the Senior High School program in the Philippines. It provides students with foundational knowledge in accounting,
business concepts, and management principles. The strand prepares students for higher education and careers in fields related to finance, business, and organizational management.
